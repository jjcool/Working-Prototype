Rules for the application

  1. The Login Id's are - User , Insurer , Reinsurer1 , Reinsurer2 .
  2. New Policy to be submitted under "New Policy" header in User page.
  3. New Claim to be sumbitted under "New Claim" header in User page.
  4. Claim automatic approved for pre-defined insurance types:
    Insurer - "Car Accident" or "Home Fire"
    Reinsurer1 - "Car Accident" or "Office Fire"
    Reinsurer2 - "Office Fire" or "Hit and Run"
  5. Claim approval can be given by Insurers by the vote button over the claim table
